community guidelines

Last Updated: September 13 2025

Chatfun is a place to connect, chat, and have fun. To keep the community safe and enjoyable, all users must follow these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, suspensions, terminations, or other enforcement actions as described in our Terms of Service.

1. Respect Others

  • Do not harass, bully, stalk, or threaten anyone.
  • Do not impersonate another person or misrepresent your identity.
  • Do not share someone else’s private information without their consent.

2. Keep It Legal

  • Do not share, request, or promote illegal content or activity.
  • Do not discuss or encourage violence, terrorism, or criminal behavior.
  • Do not engage in scams, fraud, or phishing attempts.

3. Content Standards

  • Do not post or share content that is hateful, abusive, obscene, sexually explicit, or otherwise harmful.
  • Do not spam chats, flood messages, or engage in disruptive behavior.
  • Do not share malware, viruses, or links to harmful websites.
  • Do not exploit platform features in unintended ways.

4. Global Chat Etiquette

  • Global Chat is for everyone — keep it civil, safe, and appropriate.
  • No excessive self-promotion, advertising, or off-topic spam.
  • Respect moderation decisions.

5. Direct Messages

  • Direct Messages are private but still subject to all rules.
  • Unwanted harassment, spam, or abuse in DMs will not be tolerated.
  • Respect other users’ boundaries.

6. Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for anything that happens on your account.
  • Protect your login credentials and never share them.
  • Do not create multiple accounts to evade punishments or bans.

7. Enforcement & Appeals

  • Enforcement may include mutes, suspensions, terminations, or other measures.
  • Decisions are made at Chatfun’s sole discretion.
  • Suspensions and terminations are eligible for one appeal each, as outlined in the Terms of Service.
  • Repeated or severe violations may result in permanent loss of access.

8. Reporting & Moderation

  • If you encounter violations, report them through the tools provided.
  • Do not abuse the reporting system with false or frivolous reports.
  • Moderators have the final say on enforcement.

9. Final Note

These Guidelines are here to protect the community. By using Chatfun, you agree to follow them along with the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Failure to do so may result in losing access to Chatfun permanently.
